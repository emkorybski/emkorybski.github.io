How ozone works and benefits you.

Ozone is scientifically proven as the most powerful known disinfectant (3000 faster and 50 times stronger to act than chlorine). It does what chlorine was not able to do, e.g. it destroys vicious Cryptospridium spores in water purification stations.

Ozone in high concentrations is toxic and this high concnetration is what is killing all kinds of germs in your houslehold air, mostly by reacting with (oxidizing) lipids in their cells.
Ozone is almost self-cleaning, it breaks down to regular 2-atom harmless particle after only 30 minutes.
After an hour you can start ventilation of your room to bring down ozone levels into safety level. It should be enough to give room another hour of fresh air flow to achieve that.
It is always best to hoover or wipe large surfaces in the ozone-sanitized rooms to get rid of the dead germ and other neutralized particle film.
