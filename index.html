How ozone works and benefits you.

You will typically run ozone generator in a closed/sealed off room/space that you want to disinfect or deodorize until atmosphere there is saturated with ozone (typical size bedroom or lounge does not require more than 30 minutes for that).

You generally cannot and should not enter the room for up to an hour after the ozone production/saturation run has finished. Ozone in high concentrations is toxicThen you will need 2 hours to let the ozone break down, letting a bit of fresh air inside. Hoover the floor or wipe surfaces to get rid of the dead germ film. Simple as that.

Ozone is considered more powerful than chlorine, in fact more and more swimming pools are starting to use ozone instead of chlorine.

Ozone is almost 100% eco-friendly, unlike traditional artificially produced chemical cleaning agents. It dissipates and leaves breathable oxygen as its only byproduct.
